Note: This initial study was done in a 16h research sprint as part of an application to Neel Nanda's MATS stream, Summer 2024.  

We find that SAE features do represent sufficient information about the factual recall task, and that the SAE nodes found via sparse feature circuit discovery are semantically relevant to the task. While we fall short of finding and evaluating a sparse feature circuit, these results are promising signs of life that such circuits constructed using SAE nodes might sufficiently explain model behaviour (in this task).

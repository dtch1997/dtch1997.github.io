About Me

I am primarily motivated by building a better scientific understanding of frontier AI systems. I believe that this understanding will help us build safer and more reliable AI systems.


My current research is on transparent reasoning for interpretable AI. I am currently studying chain-of-thought faithfulness, with a focus on understanding failure modes and proposing better evaluation methods.


I am currently collaborating closely with Owain Evans and his team, as part of MATS 7.0. I am also completing my PhD at University College London, where I am supervised by Paige Brooks. I am supported by a scholarship from the Agency for Science, Technology and Research (A*STAR) in Singapore.

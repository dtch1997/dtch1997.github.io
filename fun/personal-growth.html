a little verse

Stanzas on personal growth

I used to think
that life was hard
that goals were but
a fool's errand

I knew not whence
this thought had formed
But there it sat
deep in my core

It made me sad
beyond belief
It was of love
and joy a thief

if life was rigged
and doomed to fail
why even try
to win at all

i now have learned
a better creed
no limiting self
with my beliefs

instead let's try
another 'tude
that life is fun!
that effort is good

maybe I can
do all the things
maybe I can
achieve my dreams

expecting great-
ness from myself's
a drug that's hard
unlike none else
